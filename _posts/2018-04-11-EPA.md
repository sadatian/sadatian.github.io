This project features an interactive Tableau dashboard visualizing the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) data on carcinogenic chemical releases across the United States from 2014 to 2020. The visualization provides a detailed geographic and quantitative breakdown, allowing users to explore emission trends by specific companies and industry sectors.
